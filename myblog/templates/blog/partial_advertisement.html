Thank you for your interest in advertising on Across Globe!

Across Globe runs a Targeted Ad Platform with which any registered member can place adverts on any section where the people you want to reach are most likely to be found (for example, a political party could place ads on the politics section).

To place adverts,

1. Design your ad banner by an excellent graphic designer.

2. A borderless ad banner, 318 pixels wide, 106 pixels tall, less than 30kb in size, and in PNG or JPG format.

3. Once it's ready, on your left hand side upload your banner ad and landing page. Your banner will be reviewed for approval.

4. Note: Your ad might not be approved if it's deceptive, illegal or questionable.

After your ad is approved, we will provide you with information about how to purchase advertising credits so you can freely place your ads on any section of Across Globe - including the home page.

Please see our Estimated Advertising Rates to find out how much it'll cost to advertise on the various sections (prices on the short term are strongly correlated with traffic levels). Billing takes place every few minutes and you will get detailed reports on unique clicks and views and charges for each ad/section and for your entire account over various time periods.
